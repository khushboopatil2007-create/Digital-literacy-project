## Topics Covered in the Infographic

The infographic covers the following three topics:

1. **What is Digital Literacy?**
   - Definition and importance of digital literacy for students
   - Why every student needs digital skills in today's world

2. **Safe Internet Practices**
   - Creating strong passwords
   - Identifying phishing and online scams
   - Protecting personal data online

3. **Professional Online Presence**
   - Importance of platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub
   - How to present yourself professionally as a student

## Reflection 

For Task 1, I used Canva to design a one-page Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic. Canva is a free, browser-based graphic design tool that offers a wide range of ready-made templates, icons, and colour themes suitable for educational posters and infographics.

My infographic covers three key topics: what digital literacy is and why it matters for students today, safe internet practices such as creating strong passwords and identifying phishing attempts, and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence on platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub. I used a clean, colour-coded layout with short text boxes, icons, and a heading banner to make the content easy to read at a glance.

One challenge I faced was deciding how much information to include without making the design look cluttered. I found it interesting to learn that digital literacy is not just about knowing how to use devices, but also about using the internet responsibly and safely. This task helped me think about how I personally present myself online and how I can guide my peers to do the same.
